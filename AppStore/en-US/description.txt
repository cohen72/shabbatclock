The Shabbat alarm clock you've been waiting for.

Shabbat Clock is a beautifully crafted alarm clock built for observant Jewish life — with full zmanim, a Shabbat dashboard, and a clock face that looks good enough to leave on your nightstand.

Unlike other "Shabbos clocks" that rely on notifications (and give up after 30 seconds), Shabbat Clock uses Apple's system-level alarms. That means it wakes you up — every time, even on silent, even when the phone is locked, even when Do Not Disturb is on. The way an alarm is supposed to work.

ZMANIM, DONE RIGHT.
Precise halachic times for your exact location, calculated with the same engine trusted by the most popular Jewish apps. Eleven zmanim covered:

• Alot HaShachar (Dawn)
• Misheyakir (Earliest Tallit)
• Netz (Sunrise)
• Sof Zman Shma & Tefila
• Chatzot
• Mincha Gedola & Ketana
• Plag HaMincha
• Shkia (Sunset)
• Tzeis HaKochavim

Tap any zman to set an alarm before it — 10 minutes before Shkia, 30 minutes before Plag, however you daven.

A SHABBAT DASHBOARD ON YOUR HOME SCREEN.
See what you need at a glance: today's Hebrew date, this week's parasha, the countdown to Shabbat, candle lighting time, and havdalah. Whether Shabbat is tomorrow or six days away, you'll always know where you stand.

BUILT FOR THE WAY YOU ACTUALLY LIVE.
• Elegant analog clock that adapts to light and dark mode
• Warm, traditional alarm sounds — Lecha Dodi, Ana Bekoach, El Adon, and more
• Set it once, repeats every week (or just Friday nights, or only weekdays)
• Auto-stops after a reasonable time — no 10-minute sirens on Shabbos morning
• Works in English or Hebrew, with full right-to-left support
• Location-aware: precise zmanim wherever you are, from Jerusalem to Teaneck

FREE TO USE. PREMIUM WHEN YOU WANT MORE.
The free version includes three alarms and the full Shabbat dashboard — enough for most people. Shabbat Clock Premium unlocks unlimited alarms, premium alarm sounds, and custom alarm durations. Available as a weekly or yearly subscription.

A NOTE ON SHABBAT OBSERVANCE.
This app runs in the background and is designed to respect Shabbat. Set your alarms before candle lighting and put the phone down — everything keeps working without you touching it. No interaction required on Shabbat itself.

iOS 26 REQUIRED.
Shabbat Clock is built for the latest iOS and takes full advantage of AlarmKit, the new system-level alarm framework. If you're on iOS 26, you're in for a treat.
